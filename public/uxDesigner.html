About Us

eunio-aware is a company passionate about improving mental health for every person on the planet through the development of our new platform. With the right team, we envision a world where everyone is empowered to look after their mental health and find support from likeminded people in the community.

In line with this vision, eunio-aware is seeking a highly experienced UX Designer to continue to develop our platform before our near future product launch and continue to improve the platform post launch.

Responsibilities

  • • Be heavily involved in the development lifecycle of our product both pre and post launch.
  • • Finalise the user experience before launch with a focus after launch on increase usability.
  • • Increase user satisfaction through high impact and high visibility design initiatives that support the organisation’s strategies.
  • • Analyse design of competitors and market trends to ensure we are above and beyond the rest.
  • • Quantify user feedback and implement design changes based on these results.
  • • Prioritize design backlog to align with the platform’s roadmap.
